💛 About WithMe
WithMe is a support app for families of autistic children — built to give parents the right strategies, right when they need them.

Tips and strategies are grounded in Pivotal Response Treatment (PRT) and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) — two of the most evidence-based approaches available.

Every tip is written in plain language. No jargon. No pressure. Just practical help for real moments.
